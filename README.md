# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-18

This Privacy Policy explains how **Shuji** handles information in **JourneyTab**.

## Summary

JourneyTab is designed to work without requiring users to create an account or submit personal information.

The app may store app-related data locally on your device, such as settings, preferences, saved entries, or other app content. This local data stays on your device unless you choose to remove it or uninstall the app.

The app may display advertisements through third-party advertising services, such as Google AdMob. These advertising services may collect and use certain data as described in their own privacy policies.

## Information We Collect

We do not directly collect, store, or transmit personal information through our own servers.

The app may use:

- Local on-device storage or a local database for app functionality.
- Advertising SDKs to show ads.

## Local Data

The app may save data locally on your device. This may include things such as:

- App settings
- Preferences
- User-created app content
- Locally saved records

This data is stored only on your device. We do not upload this data to our own servers.

You can delete this local data by clearing the app data in your device settings or uninstalling the app.

## Advertising

JourneyTab may show ads using third-party advertising services, including Google AdMob.

Advertising providers may collect or receive certain information from your device, such as:

- Advertising ID
- Device information
- App interaction information
- Approximate location inferred from network information
- Ad performance and diagnostic data

This information may be used to provide, limit, measure, and improve ads, including personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your region, settings, and consent choices.

Google’s privacy policy is available here:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google’s information about how it uses data from apps that use its services is available here:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

## Consent for Users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland

Where required by law, users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland may be asked for consent before personalized ads are shown.

You may be able to change your ad consent choice through the app if such an option is provided, or through your device/privacy settings.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

We do not share your locally stored app data with third parties.

Third-party advertising providers may process data according to their own privacy policies and consent requirements.

## Data Security

Since app data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for protecting access to your device.

We do not operate our own backend servers for this app and do not intentionally transmit your local app data to our own systems.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Local app data remains on your device until you delete it, clear the app data, or uninstall the app.

Data processed by third-party advertising providers is handled according to their own retention and deletion policies.

## Children’s Privacy

JourneyTab is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can address the issue.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
